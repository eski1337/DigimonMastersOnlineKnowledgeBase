Quests

Free Datamon Maze Pass Card quest. (daily)
Kill 15 Gazimon. (main)
Collect 10 Red Cable from Ogremon. (main)
Find Emblem of Reliability on Ogremon. (sub-quest)
Talk to NPCs. (main)
Kill 14 Rapidmon. (main)
Collect 10 Token Of Leader from Rapidmon Leaders. (main)
Collect 10 Electricity insulator from Cyberdramon. (main)
Collect 12 High-strenght adhesive from Cyberdramon. (main)
Kill 10 Cyberdramon leaders. (sub-quest)
Talk to NPCs 2x (main)
Collect 13 Tasty fruit from Monochromon. (main)
Collect 10 Green cable from Volcamon. (main)
Kill 13 Monochromon. (main)
Kill 8 Volcamon Leaders. (sub-quest)
Kill Provoked Etemon, summoned with item in inventory. (main)
Talk with Izzy in Nanomon Maze (normal). (main)


"Final battle with Etemon" - after completing Nanomon Maze (Instance Dungeon) Normal quests (main)
Collect Emblem of Love from Gazimon in Nanomon Maze (Instance Dungeon) Normal (sub - quest)
Talk with Gennai 2x - unlocks Nanomon Maze (Instance Dungeon) Hero Mode (main)
Collect Emblem of Sincerity from Meteormon (sub - quest)
Raid - Nanomon Maze (Instance Dungeon) Hero Mode - Rewards:  Hacking File of Datamon and  Sealed Necklace of Datamon (sub - quest, daily)


 Note: Summon spot for Provoked Etemon is marked blue on map.

 
 Note: Nanomon Maze can be accessed once quest to kill Provoked Etemon is completed.
