The D-Ark Limited Edition represend 5 types of Digivice inspired by the Digimon Tamers Anime.

Each type of D-Ark Limited Edition adds a unique visual effect to your tamer.
The D-Ark Limited Edition and its previous stages can be crafted at the PawnChessmon B (DAT Member-Reward) NPC in the D-Terminal. The craft materials can be dropped from various raid bosses in the Shinjuku (D-Reaper) area.
The base version,  D-Ark Prototype Lv.1, can be obtained through a sub quest from Richard Sampson at DATS after completing all Main Quests in the Shinjuku (D-Reaper) area.
Digivice Attributes
All versions of the D-Ark Limited Edition come with 2 options. The options work similarly to the Rings, Necklaces, Earrings, and Bracelets, meaning that they can be changed with  Option Change Stone and  Number Change Stone, can be upgraded to 200% with Digitary Power Stone, and can be renewed with Renewal Increase Stone.
The 2 Digivice options can be any of the Digimon Attributes and Digimon Elements.
Unlike the Basic Attribute option in Rings, Necklaces, Earrings, and Bracelets, the Digivice Attributes are NOT damage bonus from attribute advantage. Instead, they increase the skill damage of Digimon of said Attribute. For example, the Light Attribute will increase the skill damage of every Light Digimon that you use, regardless of the enemy type matchup.
The maximum number is 10% in Digimon Attributes and 15% in Digimon Elements.
You cannot get the same option twice.
 The D-Ark Prototype Lv.3's stats are 7% Dgimon Attribute and 10% Digimon Element at 100% Digitary Power.

					<div id="mw-content-text" class="mw-body-content"><div class="mw-content-ltr mw-parser-output" lang="en" dir="ltr"><p>The <b>D-Ark Limited Edition</b> represend 5 types of Digivice inspired by the Digimon Tamers Anime. 
</p>
<ul><li>Each type of D-Ark Limited Edition adds a unique visual effect to your tamer.</li>
<li>The D-Ark Limited Edition and its previous stages can be crafted at the <a href="/PawnChessmon_B_(DAT_Member-Reward)" title="PawnChessmon B (DAT Member-Reward)">PawnChessmon B (DAT Member-Reward)</a> NPC in the <a href="/D-Terminal" title="D-Terminal">D-Terminal</a>. The craft materials can be dropped from various raid bosses in the <a href="/Shinjuku_(D-Reaper)" title="Shinjuku (D-Reaper)">Shinjuku (D-Reaper)</a> area.</li>
<li>The base version, <span class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File"><a href="/File:D-Ark_Proto_Type_Lv1-2.png" class="mw-file-description"><img src="/images/9/9b/D-Ark_Proto_Type_Lv1-2.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="32" height="32" class="mw-file-element" /></a></span> D-Ark Prototype Lv.1, can be obtained through a sub quest from <a href="/Richard_Sampson" title="Richard Sampson">Richard Sampson</a> at <a href="/DATS" class="mw-redirect" title="DATS">DATS</a> after completing all Main Quests in the <a href="/Shinjuku_(D-Reaper)" title="Shinjuku (D-Reaper)">Shinjuku (D-Reaper)</a> area.</li></ul>
<meta property="mw:PageProp/toc" />
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Digivice_Attributes">Digivice Attributes</span></h2>
<ul><li>All versions of the D-Ark Limited Edition come with 2 options. The options work similarly to the Rings, Necklaces, Earrings, and Bracelets, meaning that they can be changed with <span class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File"><a href="/File:Option_Change_Stone.png" class="mw-file-description"><img src="/images/4/44/Option_Change_Stone.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="32" height="32" class="mw-file-element" /></a></span> <b>Option Change Stone</b> and <span class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File"><a href="/File:Number_Change_Stone.png" class="mw-file-description"><img src="/images/f/f0/Number_Change_Stone.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="32" height="32" class="mw-file-element" /></a></span> <b>Number Change Stone</b>, can be upgraded to 200% with <b>Digitary Power Stone</b>, and can be renewed with <b>Renewal Increase Stone</b>.</li>
<li>The 2 Digivice options can be any of the <a href="/Digimon_Attributes" title="Digimon Attributes">Digimon Attributes</a> and <a href="/Elemental_Attributes" title="Elemental Attributes">Digimon Elements</a>.</li>
<li>Unlike the Basic Attribute option in Rings, Necklaces, Earrings, and Bracelets, the Digivice Attributes are NOT damage bonus from attribute advantage. Instead, they increase the skill damage of Digimon of said Attribute. For example, the Light Attribute will increase the skill damage of every Light Digimon that you use, regardless of the enemy type matchup.</li>
<li>The maximum number is 10% in <a href="/Digimon_Attributes" title="Digimon Attributes">Digimon Attributes</a> and 15% in <a href="/Elemental_Attributes" title="Elemental Attributes">Digimon Elements</a>.</li>
<li>You cannot get the same option twice.</li>
<li><span class="mw-default-size" typeof="mw:File"><a href="/File:D-Ark_Proto_Type_Lv2-3.png" class="mw-file-description"><img src="/images/9/95/D-Ark_Proto_Type_Lv2-3.png" decoding="async" loading="lazy" width="32" height="32" class="mw-file-element" /></a></span> The <b>D-Ark Prototype Lv.3's stats are 7% Dgimon Attribute and 10% Digimon Element at 100% Digitary Power.</b></li></ul>
