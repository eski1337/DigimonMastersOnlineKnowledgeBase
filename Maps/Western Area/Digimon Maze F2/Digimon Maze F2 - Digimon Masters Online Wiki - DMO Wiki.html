From Digimon Masters Online Wiki - DMO Wiki

A place that is believed to have been created intentionally by someone through data manipulation. The identity of the culprit is shrouded in mystery.

To unlock Digimon Maze F2, you need to complete all of the quests in Digimon Maze F1.

Quests[edit | edit source]

Room 1

  • Talk to Head Leomon | Explore the Map
  • Talk to Head Leomon | Talk to Leomon, Use items on Leomon
  • Talk to Scout Leomon | Kill 10 Ogremon, Collect 20 Ogremon's Bone Club items, Use 3 items on them
  • Talk to Scout Leomon | Kill 10 Icemon, Collect 20 Icemon's Ice Fragment items, Use 3 items on them
  • Talk to Head Leomon | Talk to Starmon, Give him papers
  • Talk to Head Leomon | Collect 20 Mini Tower Fragment Delta items from Ogremon, 20 Mini Tower Fragment Epsilon items from Icemon, Mini Tower Fragment Zeta items from Chrysalimon (Destroyer) (60 Total)
  • Talk to Head Leomon | Kill 10 Chyrsalimon, Collect 1 Garnet Crystal item


Room 2

  • Talk to Etemon | Explore the map
  • Talk to Etemon | Kill 10 Stingmons, Use 3 items on them
  • Talk to Etemon | Kill 10 Stingmon Leader, Collect 20 Stingmon Antennae items
  • Talk to Etemon | Kill 10 Vegiemons, Collect Vegiemon Insectvorous Fruit 20 items
  • Talk to Etemon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Misconnecter), Collect 20 Chrysalimon Data items
  • Talk to Etemon | Talk to Starmon and give him items
  • Talk to Etemon | Collect 20 Mini Tower Fragment Eta from Stingmon, 20 Mini Tower Fragment Theta items from Vegiemon, 20 Mini Tower Fragment Iota from Chrysalimon (Misconnecter) (60 Total)
  • Talk to Etemon | Collect 20 items from Vegiemon, Use 5 items on them
  • Talk to Etemon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Misconnecter), Collect 1 Amethyst Crystal item


Room 3

  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Explore the Map
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Kill 10 Soulmon, Use 3 items on them
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Kill 10 Soulmon, Kill 10 Grizzmon
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Kill 10 Soulmon (Leader), Collect 20 Cursed Black Hat items
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Kill 10 Grizzmon (Leader), Collect 20 Grizzmon Claw items
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Talk to Starmon and give him item
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Collect 10 items Mini Tower Fragment Kappa from Soulmon, 10 items Mini Tower Fragment Lambda from Grizzmon, 10 items Mini Tower Fragment Mu from Chrysalimon (Tentacle) (30 Total)
  • Talk to SuperStarmon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Tentacle), Collect 1 Aquamarine Crystal item


Room 4

  • Talk to Starmon | Kill 10 Seadramon, Collect 20 Aritfact Map items
  • Talk to Starmon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Fighter), Collect 1 Anubis Silver Bracelet item (In Fourth Room)
  • Talk to Starmon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Tentacle), Collect 1 Anubis Silver Armband item (In Third Room)
  • Talk to Starmon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Misconnector), Collect 1 Anubis Silver Necklace item (In Second Room)
  • Talk to Starmon | Kill 10 Chrysalimon (Destroyer), Collect 1 Anubis Silver Shoes item (In First Room)
  • Talk to Starmon | Summon Guardromon Boss, Kill Guardromon Boss
  • Talk to Anubismon | Kill 10 Chyrsalimon (Fighter), Kill 10 Seadramon, Kill 10 Seadramon (Leader)
  • Talk to Anubismon | Talk to Starmon

> Move to Digimon Maze F3
